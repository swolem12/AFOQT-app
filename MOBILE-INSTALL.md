# Mobile Installation Guide

## Quick Start - Install on Your Phone (No APK Needed!)

The **fastest** way to get AFOQT Quest on your Android phone is to install it directly from your browser. This takes less than 30 seconds!

### Android Installation Steps:

1. **Open Chrome** on your Android phone
   
2. **Visit**: [https://swolem12.github.io/AFOQT-app/](https://swolem12.github.io/AFOQT-app/)

3. **Look for the install prompt**:
   - Chrome may show an "Add AFOQT Quest to Home screen" banner at the bottom
   - OR tap the menu (â‹®) in the top-right and select **"Install app"** or **"Add to Home screen"**

4. **Tap "Install"** or **"Add"**

5. **Done!** The app icon now appears on your home screen

6. **Launch** the app from your home screen - it opens in fullscreen without browser UI

### iPhone (iOS) Installation Steps:

1. **Open Safari** on your iPhone
   
2. **Visit**: [https://swolem12.github.io/AFOQT-app/](https://swolem12.github.io/AFOQT-app/)

3. **Tap the Share button** (square with arrow pointing up)

4. **Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"**

5. **Tap "Add"** in the top-right

6. **Launch** the app from your home screen

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do I need to download an APK file?
**A:** No! The PWA installation method above is easier and works just as well as an APK. The app installs directly from the website.

### Q: Will this use mobile data?
**A:** Only for the initial installation. After that, the app works 100% offline.

### Q: How much storage does it use?
**A:** Approximately 5-10 MB - much smaller than most apps.

### Q: Can I uninstall it later?
**A:** Yes! Just long-press the icon and select "Uninstall" or "Remove", just like any other app.

### Q: Will I get updates?
**A:** Yes! Updates happen automatically when you're online. No need to download anything.

### Q: What if I still want an APK file?
**A:** See [APK-SETUP.md](APK-SETUP.md) for instructions on creating an APK using PWABuilder or Bubblewrap.

### Q: Does this work on tablets?
**A:** Yes! The app is responsive and works great on tablets, phones, and desktops.

### Q: What browsers are supported?
**A:** 
- **Android**: Chrome, Edge, Samsung Internet, Firefox
- **iOS**: Safari (limited PWA support, but works)
- **Desktop**: Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari

## Troubleshooting

### "Install" or "Add to Home Screen" option is missing

**On Android:**
- Make sure you're using Chrome or Edge
- Wait a few seconds after the page loads
- Check that you're visiting via HTTPS (not HTTP)
- Try tapping the three-dot menu (â‹®) and look for "Install app"

**On iPhone:**
- Make sure you're using Safari (not Chrome)
- Use the Share button (square with up arrow)
- Scroll down in the share sheet to find "Add to Home Screen"

### App doesn't work offline

- Visit the app at least once while online to cache the files
- Check that you have enough storage space on your device
- Make sure you didn't clear your browser cache immediately after installing

### App icon doesn't appear

- Check your app drawer or home screen pages
- On Android, it might be in the app drawer instead of the home screen
- Try the installation process again

### App looks different than website

- This is normal! The installed app runs in fullscreen mode without browser UI
- This makes it feel more like a native app
